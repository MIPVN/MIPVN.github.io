Welcome to MIPVN Lab!

Multimodal Information Perception and Visual Navigation Lab is a research group directed by Prof. Xiaoyan Luo at Image Processing Center, School of Astronautics, Beihang University, China.

Research foucus:

Multimodal Information Perception.
Visual language Navigation.
Remote Sensing Image Processing.
Medical Image Processing.

Recruitment and Incentives:

We warmly welcome students with a pioneering and innovative spirit, as well as a diligent and studious attitude, to join our research group for graduate-level research and undergraduate research intenship. The laboratory provides generous and diverse research subsidies and team bonuses. Various opportunities for academic and technical exchanges at home and abroad are provided for master's and doctoral students, with the laboratory covering related expenses.
